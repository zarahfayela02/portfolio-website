About me

Hi! I´m Zarah-Faye La, a graphic designer with a background in interior design. I started my creative journey by completing a higher technical college diploma (HTL) in Interior Design. Thanks to the broad curriculum, I quickly discovered my passion for graphic design — a path that has shaped my work ever since. To deepen my skills, I pursued a Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design in the UK, where I was able to explore visual communication in depth and refine my personal style. Currently, I’m expanding my knowledge through a UX design course — because for me, great design is not just about how things look, but how they work and feel to the user. I’m excited to keep creating thoughtful, functional, and visually strong design solutions.
