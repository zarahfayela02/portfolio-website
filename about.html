About Me

My Story

Hello, my name is Zarah-Faye La, and I am based in Vienna, Austria.

I come from a background in Interior and Surface Design as well as Graphic Design, which has given me a strong foundation in visual storytelling, layout, and creative problem-solving. Over time, I realized that I am particularly passionate about designing experiences that are both visually appealing and user-friendly, which led me to expand my skills into the field of UX and UI design.

Currently, I am focused on developing my expertise as a Graphic/UX Designer, combining my design sensibility with user-centered thinking. I enjoy the entire design process — from research and ideation to wireframing, prototyping, and testing — and I enjoy turning ideas and insights into easy-to-use and attractive designs.

My multidisciplinary background allows me to approach projects with both an artistic eye and a practical mindset, ensuring that the solutions I create are functional, accessible, and engaging. I thrive in collaborative environments and am motivated by projects that challenge me to grow, work across disciplines, and design with real impact in mind.
